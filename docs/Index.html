2017-12-20

Welcome

The fact that you are here suggest you have an interest to do population modeling in R ! Maybe you found this through our nlmixr website. Maybe via Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub. Either way: welcome and join us in our journey to do population modeling in R !

This book is intended to get you started with nlmixr: an R package for population PKPD modeling. You’ll get information on the package itself, its installation, interesting use cases and more.
